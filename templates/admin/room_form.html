Room Type Guidelines
Single Room
  • 1 student per room
  • Private space
  • Recommended fee: RS50,000 per semester
  • Typically assigned to final year students
Double Room
  • 2 students per room
  • Shared space
  • Recommended fee: RS35,000 per semester
  • Good for most students
Triple Room
  • 3 students per room
  • Shared space
  • Recommended fee: RS25,000 per semester
  • Economical option for students
Note: Ensure room capacity matches the selected room type. Single rooms should have capacity 1, double rooms should have capacity 2, and triple rooms should have capacity 3.

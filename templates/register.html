Registration Information

By registering, you're creating a student account for the IST Smart Hostel System. After registration:

  1. You'll need to complete your student profile with personal details.
  2. You can apply for hostel accommodation at the beginning of each semester.
  3. Your application will be reviewed by the hostel administration.
  4. If approved, you'll be allocated a room based on availability and your preferences.
Registration is for student accounts only. If you need an admin account, please contact the system administrator.
